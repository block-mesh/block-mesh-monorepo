What is BlockMesh Network?

BlockMesh Network serves as the backbone for powering AI models. You benefit from the growth of AI, earning a share in the network by contributing a resource you may not have realized you possessed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe, secure and private?

Your Personal Data is Protected: BlockMesh is solely a network for contributing bandwidth, with no access granted to your personal data or online activity. Our open-source platform ensures total security and privacy, backed by transparent, community-driven oversight that guarantees your protection

What is BlockMesh Network

BlockMesh Network is a decentralized, open-source data network where you can easily monetize your excess bandwidth and join the forefront of AI and online privacy

How Can You Participate?

By running a BlockMesh node, you can contribute to the network by allowing it to occasionally utilize your idle bandwidth to route web requests. This process runs quietly in the background, without impacting your internet experience or requiring any active involvement from you.
