BlockMesh Network - Refer to Earn

You can earn points with BlockMesh Network in multiple ways

  1. Participating in the network
  2. Referrals
  3. Contributing



Participating in the network
This is the simplest form, install one of BlockMesh Network clients (chrome extension/desktop/mobile) and it will automatically run in the background and earn points
Referrals
  1. Main Referrals: Will increase your points accrual by +20% of your main points
  2. Secondary Referrals: Will increase your points accrual by +10% of the secondary points
  3. Descendants Referrals:Will increase your points accrual by +5% of the descendants points
Bonus for referrals
  1. 500 points bonus when someone you referred reaches 75 hours of uptime
  2. 250 points bonus for the referred person when they reach 75 hours of uptime
Level up
After reaching each milestone, you will level up.

Contribute

We launch bounties regularly for art/branding/programming/instructional videos and more. You can participate in the bounties or contribute directly via discord , GitHub or any other way. Good and talented people are always welcomed.

Caveats
  • Commissions/bonuses are subject to change.
  • Commissions/bonuses require a minimum of 75 hours of uptime.
